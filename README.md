### ✨About me
I am currently in my second semester of third year in Software Engineering at Ontario Tech University and have completed a year-long co-op with Fidelity Canada, rotating across Fidelity Clearing Canada ULC (January–June 2025) and Fidelity Investments Canada ULC (July–December 2025). During this co-op, I contributed to a range of projects in IT and data architecture, including building an automated architecture engagement intake process using Microsoft Forms and ServiceNow to streamline submissions and track engagement metadata, developing a proof-of-concept for session management containerized with Docker and deployed on Kubernetes to ensure scalable and persistent request handling, and supporting SSL certificate renewal processes to maintain secure and compliant systems. I also built a dashboard in Snowflake by integrating data from Jira and Eclipse PPM to provide real-time visibility into engagement metrics, prepared architecture blueprints and technical documentation while maintaining the Architecture repository in Confluence, and led Architecture Review Board submissions for a new product feature serving 8,000 users as well as onboarding a third-party application serving 20 users. Additionally, I updated and validated ServiceNow metadata, collaborated with Fidelity teams to maintain the FCC Application Portfolio, coordinated with the Data Protection Office to ensure compliance during third-party onboarding, and managed a Jira board with automation rules to streamline project tracking. This co-op strengthened my skills in enterprise architecture, cloud technologies, data integration, and process automation, while giving me hands-on experience working in a professional, enterprise environment.
